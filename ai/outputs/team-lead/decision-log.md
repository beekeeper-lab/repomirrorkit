## Guidance

- **When to log**: Any choice that affects scope, architecture, process, or team assignments.
- **When to write a full ADR**: If the decision is hard to reverse, affects multiple teams, or involves significant trade-offs.
- **Review cadence**: Review this log at the start of each wave or sprint to confirm all active decisions still hold.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Every significant decision has an entry with rationale
- [ ] Complex decisions link to a full ADR
- [ ] Superseded decisions are clearly marked with their replacement
- [ ] Log reviewed at each wave boundary
